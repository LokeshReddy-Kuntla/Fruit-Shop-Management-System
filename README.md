

# Fruit Shop Management System

A **Python-based console application** that helps manage a fruit shop.  
It allows both the **shop owner** and **customers** to interact through a simple text-based menu system — including inventory management, billing, and sales reports.


## Features

### Owner Section
- **Add items** – Add new fruits with quantity, cost price, and selling price.  
- **Remove items** – Remove fruits that are no longer available.  
- **Update items** – Modify quantity or prices of existing fruits.  
- **View inventory** – Display all available fruits with their details.  
- **Sales report** – View total sales, profit, and sold item history.  
- **Customer details** – View all customers, their purchases, and bills.

### User (Customer) Section
- **Add to cart** – Select fruits and quantity to purchase.  
- **Remove from cart** – Remove fruits from the shopping cart.  
- **Modify cart** – Change the quantity of fruits in the cart.  
- **View cart** – Display items added to the cart.  
- **Billing** – Generate a detailed bill with total cost and quantity.
